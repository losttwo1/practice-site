Ethical Hacking Practice

About This Site

This website is designed to help you learn the fundamentals of ethical hacking and cybersecurity awareness.

Use it only for legal training, education, and improving defensive skills. Never test systems without permission.

Ethical Principles

  • Always obtain explicit permission before testing a system.
  • Respect privacy and confidentiality.
  • Use your skills to protect systems, not harm them.
